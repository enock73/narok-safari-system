How to Book Your Safari

Get your official gate clearance ticket in 4 easy steps — fully digital, secure, and instant.

1
👤
Create Account

Register as a tour guide with your name, ID, and KWS licence number. Takes 2 minutes.

2
🚙
Register Vehicle

Add your safari vehicle — Land Cruiser, Land Rover, Minivan — with plate number and insurance details.

3
🎫
Request Ticket

Select your gate, entry date, add all passengers, and submit your gate clearance request.

4
📱
Get QR Code

Once approved by the county office, download your QR code ticket and present it at the gate.
